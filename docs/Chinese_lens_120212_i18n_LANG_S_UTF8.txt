patent.type.Statutory_Invention_Registration	"An inventor wishing to prevent others from obtaining a patent on their invention may request, at any time during the pendency of a completed patent application, that the specification and drawings be published as a Statutory Invention Registration (SIR). To have the SIR published the inventor waives all patent rights to the subject matter claimed in the statutory invention registration, but its publication prevents others from obtaining patent rights on that invention, anywhere in the world that recognises the SIR, from any patent applications with priority dates after the date of publication of the SIR."	任何希望能防止他人获得自己的专利发明权的发明人可以在专利申请未决期间的任何时间里要求将说明书和图纸作为法定发明登记（SIR）公开。这样做的话，发明人必须放弃所有与法定发明登记相关的专利权项，但是该公开行为可以阻止其他想要获得此发明专利的人进行申请。此公开在世界范围内任何承认法定发明登记（sir)的地区都有效。 在法定发明登记里公布后，任何专利申请都享有优先权。
